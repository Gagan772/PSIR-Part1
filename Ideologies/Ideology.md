# Ideology — General Topic

---

## Origin

- Term **Ideology** coined by **Claude de Tracy**
- Meaning: **“Science of ideas”**

---

## Critics of Ideology (General)

- **Michel Foucault** → No ideology free from knowledge–power connection  
- **Karl Marx** → Ruling class ideas = ruling ideas  
- **Hannah Arendt** → Ideology used by totalitarian states to justify terror  
- **Karl Mannheim** → Sociology of knowledge; every theory reflects class position  
- **Lyotard** → Ideologies are meta-narratives  

---

# End of Ideology Thesis

### Daniel Bell

- Called himself: “socialist in economics, liberal in politics, conservative in culture”
- East & West show similar political/administrative development
- Rise of bureaucratic & technocratic societies
- Ideologies losing relevance

### S.M. Lipset

- Shift from ideological politics → politics of development  
- Post-war West solved problems of industrial revolution → less need for ideology  

### W.W. Rostow

- Modernisation theory  
- Book: *Stages of Economic Growth: A Non-Communist Manifesto*  
- All states pass similar growth stages regardless of ideology  

### Milovan Djilas

- Concept of **New Class** → Bureaucracy holds power everywhere  

---

## Critics of End of Ideology

1. **C. Wright Mills** → West hasn’t resolved social conflicts  
2. **C.B. Macpherson** → Wealth inequality persists  
3. **Ralph Miliband** → Justifies status quo; welfare state is myth  
4. **Alasdair MacIntyre** → End of ideology is itself ideology; supports Western liberal democracy as ideal  

---

# Francis Fukuyama

### Liberalism and its Discontents

- Liberalism doesn’t tell people how to live  
- Creates moral vacuum → filled by consumerism/pop culture  

---

# End of History Thesis (1989)

- End of Cold War = end of ideological evolution  
- Western liberal democracy = final form of government  

### Influences

- Hegel’s dialectics  
- Alexandre Kojève → Universal homogeneous state  
- George Kennan → Spiritual vitality  
- Christian myth idea of universal order  

---

## Criticism of End of History

### Benjamin Barber

- Clash between corporate culture & religious orthodoxy  

### Seumas Milne — *Revenge of History*

- Western states support authoritarian regimes  
- Liberalism also violent  

### Samuel Huntington — Clash of Civilisations

- Ideological conflict replaced by civilisational conflict  
- Identity replaces ideology  

#### Relevance

- Weakening liberal order  
- China challenge  
- Azerbaijan–Armenia conflict  
- Asian values debate  
- Post-2001 global conflicts  
- 2008 GFC dented liberal credibility  
- Identity politics rising  

#### Criticism of COC

- Too simplistic  
- Civil wars within same civilisation (Sudan, Yemen, Shia–Sunni)  
- Used to justify US hegemony  
- Abraham Accords show geopolitics > civilisation  

---

## New End of History

- Asian democratic technocracy model  
- Japan, South Korea, Taiwan examples  
- Mix of technocracy + capitalism + social conservatism  
- Biden → democracy vs autocracy narrative  

---

## Conclusion

- Postmodernists (Lyotard): All ideologies are discourses  
- No ideology represents absolute truth
